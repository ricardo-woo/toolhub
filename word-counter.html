Frequently asked questions

How many words is a 5-minute read? +
At an average adult reading speed of 200 words per minute, a 5-minute read is approximately 1,000 words. This tool calculates reading time based on that standard rate.
Does this count characters with or without spaces? +
Both. The tool shows total characters (including spaces) and characters without spaces separately. Most social media platforms count characters including spaces, while some writing tools exclude them.
Is my text stored or sent anywhere? +
No. All counting happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your text is never sent to any server. You can even use this tool offline once the page has loaded.
How are sentences counted? +
Sentences are counted by detecting ending punctuation: periods, exclamation marks, and question marks. This works well for standard prose but may not perfectly handle abbreviations or ellipses.
